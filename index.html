Instructions:

1. Click or tap the blue dots as quickly as possible

2. Each dot will appear for a short time

3. Try to tap all 10 dots that appear

Reaction Time Test
Instructions:

1. Wait for the screen to turn green

2. Click anywhere on the screen as soon as it turns green

3. Do NOT click before it turns green (this will restart the test)

4. You will complete 3 trials for an average reaction time
