Our Mission

NeuroSketch combines traditional clinical evaluation methods with cutting-edge AI technology to provide comprehensive, accurate, and accessible neurological screening. Our platform democratizes access to neurological assessment, making early detection possible for everyone.

Key Features:
  • Multi-modal assessment tests
  • Real-time analysis and reporting
  • Secure patient data management
  • Professional-grade accuracy
  • Voice-controlled interface
  • Comprehensive result interpretation
Clinical Validation

Our assessment tools have been validated through extensive clinical studies and are designed in collaboration with leading neurologists and researchers in the field of movement disorders.
